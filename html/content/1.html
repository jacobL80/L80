Problem Statement

The purpose of this project was to create a label for a number of flavors of vape juice for a small start-up company.

Goals

"Hand-made"

The client specifically requested a design that feels hand-crafted, so illustrator/vectoring should be avoided to keep things from looking too "clean".

"Local"

The client additionally requested a design that is evocative of the Pacific Northwest, so imagery that inspires that should be utilized.

Solution

Logo Design

In order to best evoke the essence of the Pacific Northwest, I made a logo that included an ample amount of trees, a mountain, and both sun and rain simultaneously. In order to make the design feel more hand-crafted, I wrote the text out by hand instead of using a font face.
