Problem Statement

The purpose of this school project was to create a mock up for a proposed addition to the University's suite of websites.

Goals

Fun/Interesting

The design should be quirky and off the wall to help make it stand out from other submissions.

UW Themed

The design should utilize UW branding guidelines and feel like it could realistically be a new addition to their suite of websites, while also being a concept that appeals to UW students.

Sketch

Conceptualization

While brainstorming for this project, one of the first things that came to mind was the parody title- Hungry, Hungry Huskies. I expanded from this idea outwards- what sort of service would have a name like this? It would have to be something related to food in order to tie the concept together better. I came upon the idea, then, of an application/service that would allow users to not only track their current pantries contents, but collaborate with neighbors to communally create dishes together. Thus, the initial concept was born.
The initial, somewhat joke-y sketch of the layout.

Mock Ups

Using the sketch I had made, I formalized the design in Illustrator, making sure to use official UW branding guidelines (the color scheme, logo, and general website layout). I created 3 different versions: one with a persistant sidebar, one with a dropdown menu, and one with iconography in place of text.
