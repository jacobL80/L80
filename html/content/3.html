Problem Statement

The purpose of this school project was to create an infographic about my subject of choice- in this case, the video game League of Legends.

Goals

Fun/Interesting

This is an infographic about a video game, so its layout and design should be fun and interesting to reflect the subject matter.

Colorful

The source material for this infographic is very colorful, so the inforgraphic should reflect that.

Insightful

People who read this infographic should learn something new about the game, even if they already know about it and/or play it.

Color Scheme

Colors

A palette of vibrant and slightly pastel-like colors was chosen to reflect the fun and whimsical nature of the game. Brown was selected as a primary background color to reflect the game's setting of a mountain-top forest.
The color palette for the infographic.

Editing Based on Feedback

Player Distribution

Based on peer feedback, the pie chart was determined to be more compelling in displaying this data, as one could more easily compare ranks to one another but also get a better feel for what its quantity was relative to the whole player base.
Before and after peer-reviewed edits.
Before and after peer-reviewed edits.

Classes by Position

On personal review, the rigid lines felt unnatural in this part of the infographic, so I opted for more of a diagram-based graphic, which I felt gave the piece more of a "fun" feel.

Count by Class

It became clear after peer review that the comparative bubble chart was not a satisfying way of comparing the champion class count, so I instead opted for a more simple visualization, which overall improved the piece by emphasizing just how many options there were per grouping and gave more meaning to the total count.
Before and after peer-reviewed edits.
Before and after peer-reviewed edits.

Release Dates

In another personal review decision, I changed the timeline to have a more coiled effect to both better take up space on the infographic, but also to make viewing what was one of the more simple visualizations more interesting by inviting the viewer to follow the cords turns.
