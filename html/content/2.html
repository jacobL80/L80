Problem Statement

The purpose of my work with the UW club Purple Caster Minions was to help facilitate a brand while creating fun and cutesy illustrations for use in advertising and on their website.

Logo

Design

The PCM team decided early on that the Purple Caster Minions should be represented by just that- a purple caster minion from the game. In order to tie this in with the University better, it was determined that the two mascots-- the minion and UW's husky-- should be combined together for the club's new logo. I proposed a coat of arms-styled logo initially, but after feedback was collected went with a more formal logo as seen to the right.
The initial and final logo designs

T-Shirt

A t-shirt design was created in order to help generate revenue for the club and unite club members around a common item. The design was chosen out of several because it not only reflected the club's mascot via the clothing on the character depicted, but was also deemed the "cutest" of all.
