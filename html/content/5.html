Background

Sponsored by Microsoft’s Technology and Civic Engagement Department, SPSInteractive is a data-driven aggregate that allows users to compare various data points about Seattle Public Schools to one another, created with the intent of allowing policy makers make more informed decisions on school funding, and parents on which schools to send their children. It won the “Social Impact Award” and $1250 at the 2016 Informatics Capstone Night.
